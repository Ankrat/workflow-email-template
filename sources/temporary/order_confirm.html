How long until my order is dispatched?

If your item is in stock, it will be picked either today or the next working day. Most of our stock is picked from our Oxford bookshop, but it could come from other locations across the UK or US. Our CD's will be sent from our music shop in Cambridge.

If your item is not in stock, we order from the publisher. Most publishers dispatch the item to us within 48 hours and we will then send immediately on to you. This is also pretty typical of the classical CD market, too.

Due to the highly specialist requirements of our customers, we do source books from an extensive range of publishers and music labels. Some titles are more difficult to source and these orders can take longer to dispatch.

We will notify you by e mail as soon as your order is dispatched.
